Ceramic Coating Services

Nairobi, Kenya

Shield your car with premium ceramic coatings, lasting protection from scratches, UV, chemicals, with a slick, glossy finish.

Ceramic coating is an advanced liquid protection system that chemically bonds with your vehicle’s paintwork to create a permanent protective layer. At Urban X Detail, we use certified ceramic coatings that form a hard, glass-like surface providing superior protection against UV rays, chemicals, scratches, and environmental contaminants.

Our ceramic coatings offer extreme hydrophobic properties causing water to bead and roll off seamlessly. This makes maintenance cleaning significantly easier, drastically reduces water spotting, and gives your vehicle a permanent showroom finish.

Professional Detailing

Nairobi, Kenya

Restore your vehicle’s shine with paint correction, interior protection, & professional detailing using premium products.

Car detailing goes beyond your regular wash. It’s a full service to clean, protect and freshen up your car’s paint, wheels, trims, and interior. At Urban X Detail, our vehicle detailing services include paint decontamination, single-stage polish, and interior cleaning, designed to remove light swirls, dullness, and road grime.

We use professional tools, techniques, and premium products for a safe, quality result. From soft blow drying to clay bar treatment and sealant protection, every step is done with care and attention. Choose from our Paint Enhancement Package or Full Interior and Exterior Detail.
